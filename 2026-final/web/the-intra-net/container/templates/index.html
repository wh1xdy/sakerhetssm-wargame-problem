💕 About The Fence Authority

The Fence Authority was founded in 1847 by royal decree and has since worked tirelessly to ensure that every citizen has access to fences of the highest quality.

Our operations are governed by the Fence Act (SFS 1847:42) and the Regulation on Enclosures and Boundaries (SFS 1923:17). We operate under the Ministry of Enterprise.

The agency's mandate includes:

  • Providing fences to individuals and businesses
  • Conducting quality control of fence manufacturing
  • Managing the national fence registry
  • Conducting research in modern fence technology

🌞 Our Services

🌳 Wooden Fences

Classic wooden fences in pine and spruce. Available in heights of 80 cm, 120 cm, and 180 cm. All wooden fences are FSC-certified.

🔨 Metal Fences

Robust metal fences in galvanized steel. Suitable for industry, agriculture, and public spaces. 25-year rust warranty.

🌾 Rustic Fences

Traditional rustic fences crafted according to artisan traditions. Perfect for rural settings and heritage properties.

🌹 Hedge Solutions

Living fences in the form of hedge plantings. We offer privet, boxwood, and hornbeam with planting service.
