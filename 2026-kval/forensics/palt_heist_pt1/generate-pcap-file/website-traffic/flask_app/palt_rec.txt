TOP SECRET RECIPE

Potatos: 1kg
Flour: 4dl
Salt: 1tsk

Salted pork: 300g

Served with butter and lingonberry jam.

1. Peel the potatoes and cut the pork
2. Stir the potatoes, flour and salt.
3. Cut the dough into pieces and make a well. Inside the well place the pork.
4. Roll the Palt to round balls.
5. Boil in some water
